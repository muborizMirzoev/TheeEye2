Our company's founders have worked in the global real estate market for over 20 years, and have a deep understanding of all of it's problems.

  • Time has become the primary resource of our civilization in the course of its' development. Today we waste hours, days or even weeks in search of a nice home or office. Tomorrow, with Theeye, we'll be able to view all the best options in any city without leaving our home or office, choose one and settle in. Make your cup in advance because the broadcast goes live in 30 seconds!
  • All of today's methods of relaying information about a real estate property have inevitably aged. Each of has experienced seeing one thing in a property's photos and then being let down when visiting the place physically. Tomorrow you'll be able to look at all of the smallest details, ask all of your questions and discuss everything in real-time online.
  • A singular global marketplace will help you feel like you're in another country, at home. You'll be able to visit your favorite property without the need to look for real estate websites of other countries, learn another language or divide sqft by m^2. It's very simple: Choose a city, a couple of filters and enjoy the experience of live broadcast.

Purchacing any product, communication, learning and many other processes online and through streaming have gained a massive increase in popularity lately and in the years 2020-2021 specifically, on a global scale. Our team is creating a new product for future generations, which will live in a world without borders and become the first to be citizens of the world. We're creating a world where watching and aquiring a property, or similarly move to any point in the world will be as simple as ordering a pizza or a taxi with a single click, today.
