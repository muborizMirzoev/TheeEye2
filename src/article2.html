There are several traditional ways of relaying information about a real estate property in all real estate marketplaces:

  • Photos don't alwats represent the most current information of a property and aren't capable of accurately representing the spaciousness, and negative aspects often go unnoticed or are even hidden, becoming an unpleasant surprise until a physical visit and inspection of the place.
  • 3D tours of a real estate property don't fully give the impression of being in the property itself, or of the spaciousness. The adjacent exterior is also often not represented.
  • Live streaming of real estate properties - a new direction in the world of real estate and a new model of use.

Live broadcasting is principally different from all other methods of aquiring information mainly by always being in communication with a second country and by making direct descisions in the process of that communication, enabling the viewer to be sure about the smallest detail. In the process of viewing the property you're able to ask the streamer to show you the surrounding space, look out the window, stand in place for a minute and imagine yourself inside the property, remember a question you had and immediately find out about a crucial detail and discuss terms with the presenter in the country you're viewing the property in.

Live broadcasting is becoming a new global trend in e-commerce. Some countries are already using live broadcasting to sell various products. The same products eventually reach up to 30% of viewers!

Our company believes that live broadcasting not only minimizes the time it takes to search for a real estate property, but makes the experience pleasant and comfortable. VR and 5G technologies present the opportunity for the users of our platform to fully experience being in the property in the future.
